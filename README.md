# AVIATION-WORKFORCE-HEALTH-STUDY
A data analysis project exploring BMI and health indicators among employees in an aviation company.

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the health metrics of aviation employees, focusing on BMI and other wellness indicators to identify trends that may impact employee well-being and workplace performance.

## Business Problem
Aviation organizations need to ensure their employees maintain good health and fitness because poor health conditions, such as high BMI and High Blood Pressure, can affect productivity, job performance and safety in aviation operations. However, there is limited analysis of employee health data to identify potential health risks within the workforce.

## Project Objective
To examine how employees' demographic details, job roles, and basic health indicators relate to their fitness status, and identify possible health risks that could affect safety and performance of employees.
